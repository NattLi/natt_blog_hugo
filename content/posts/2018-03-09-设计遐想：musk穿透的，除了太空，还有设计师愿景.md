<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703">那位长者说过：“一个人的命运啊，当然要靠自我奋斗，但是也要考虑到历史的行程”</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">。</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">对，我们总是处在时代之中</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">：</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">手工艺</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">者</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">赶上工业时代孕育出工业设计师；工业设计师赶上信息时代孕育出交互与体验设计师。</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">时代为我们的经历圈定了</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">一个特定的范围和前提。</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-5022786">如果</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">，</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">一旦</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">这个前提被彻底改变或颠覆了会怎样？本文让我们跟随Elon Musk 的火箭直插云霄，去探讨和构想一个新的愿景。</span>
</p>

<h2 class="ql-align-center ql-long-4782703" style="text-align: center;">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703 ql-size-14">· 宏大与渺小 ·</span>
</h2>

<div id="gallery_image_2" class="ql-blot-gallery-block ql-blot-gallery ql-gallery-margin-none ql-align-center ql-long-4782703 ql-authorBlock-4782703 ql-gallery-frame-none" contenteditable="false">
</div>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6056" src="https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/1.jpg" alt="" width="1000" height="562" srcset="https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/1.jpg 1000w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/1-450x253.jpg 450w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/1-600x337.jpg 600w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/1-300x169.jpg 300w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/1-768x432.jpg 768w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" />Elon Musk</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">又让这个世界</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">沸腾了一波</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">。</span><span class="ql-author-4782703"> 他用自己公司的火箭把自己公司的汽车送上火星轨道，不出意外，假人将驾驶着Tesla 在火星轨道运行10亿年。</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">Amazing！！！！！只是没想到</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">，</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">火箭的</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">助推器完成使命</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">后</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">，竟然</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">被</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">回收了。嗯，这很节俭。这样的</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">“</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">成就</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">”</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">，还需要用收入、估值、上市等标准来衡量</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">，感觉价值观都要</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">被颠覆了。</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703">高晓松说，生活不止眼前的苟且，还有诗和远方</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">的田野</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">。Musk 为人类描绘的浩瀚宇宙</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">，</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">是我们尚未看清但充满期待的未知世界。当我们还在纠结哪个专业更好</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">，</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">哪个学校更好</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">，</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">北京房价</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">太高，中年是否会焦虑</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">的时候，这一支火箭将我们的思绪从身边拉起，直插浩瀚宇宙。</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703">在这样宏伟的愿景之下，我们</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">的</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">日常均变成了苟且，</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">那</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">诗和远方在哪里？</span>
</p>

<h2 class="ql-align-center ql-long-4782703" style="text-align: center;">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703 ql-size-14">· 设计源假设的崩塌 ·</span>
</h2>

<div id="gallery_image_3" class="ql-blot-gallery-block ql-blot-gallery ql-gallery-margin-none ql-align-center ql-long-4782703 ql-authorBlock-4782703 ql-gallery-frame-none" contenteditable="false">
</div>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6057" src="https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/2.jpg" alt="" width="1000" height="500" srcset="https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/2.jpg 1000w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/2-450x225.jpg 450w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/2-600x300.jpg 600w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/2-300x150.jpg 300w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/2-768x384.jpg 768w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/2-670x335.jpg 670w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" />Musk 的宏大愿景虽不至于摧毁人类现在的世界观和使命感，但确实</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">给了每一个人</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">仰望星空的理由，让人们反思甚至怀疑自己的追求是否太过渺小</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">。</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">这样的撼动</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">哪怕只有一点点，可能都将</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">强力地扩展人类的思维边界。</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703">任何学科都有一个源头的假设</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">：</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">先预设 1+1 =2， 才有了后续“计算”概念的出现和发展</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">；</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">假设人们都是理性的，</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">经济学</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">才有了后续繁荣而复杂的金融和经济</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">。</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">那设计的源命题呢？对，就是我们每个设计师大学伊始学习的</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">——</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">设计是解决问题的。</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703">如同一只火箭极大拓展我们对于宇宙的愿景，那设计的愿景呢？</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">如果</span><strong class="ql-author-4782703">设计愿景</strong><strong class="ql-author-5022786">足够宏大，是不是</strong><strong class="ql-author-4782703">能够撼动</strong><strong class="ql-author-5022786">甚至</strong><strong class="ql-author-4782703">摧毁设计的源命题呢</strong><span class="ql-author-4782703">？试想</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">，</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">若干年后，设计师面对的不再是用户的问题和痛点(problems & painpoints)，而必须带着独特而敏锐的洞察(insight)，去引导用户的行为、体验、期待，</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">乃</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">至思维方式和世界观</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">，那究竟会变成一番怎样的景象。</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-5022786">设</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">计师不再面对用户问题</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">的</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">时候，那将是怎样</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">的茫然和无助</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">，如同深处漩涡之中</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">，</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">身边</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">的</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">世界轰然倒塌。</span>
</p>

<h2 class="ql-align-center ql-long-4782703" style="text-align: center;">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703 ql-size-14">· 新的遐想 ·</span>
</h2>

<div id="gallery_image_4" class="ql-blot-gallery-block ql-blot-gallery ql-gallery-margin-none ql-align-center ql-long-4782703 ql-authorBlock-4782703 ql-gallery-frame-none" contenteditable="false">
</div>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703"><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6058" src="https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/3.jpg" alt="" width="1000" height="750" srcset="https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/3.jpg 1000w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/3-450x338.jpg 450w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/3-600x450.jpg 600w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/3-300x225.jpg 300w, https://blog.natt.cc/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/3-768x576.jpg 768w" sizes="auto, (max-width: 1000px) 100vw, 1000px" />试想，我们能够如同达利一般看到之前所不能看到的，构想之前所不能构想的，期待之前所不能期待的，那么这样大破大立的世界，设计师不再为“问题”服务，将</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">构造</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">一个</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">什么样的</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">设计愿景？</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <strong class="ql-author-4782703">遐想，设计师将会转变为一个生活方式的体验者与布道者。</strong>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703">设计师不再是以一个修补匠的身份修补这个世界，而是如同那一枚火箭穿越了时间，去创造与体验新的生活方式，去寻求新的生活可能，去为人类探索新的方向，并坚定为之奋袖呐喊，以一种具备未来洞察力的姿态引导世界。</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <strong class="ql-author-4782703">总结</strong>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703">交互设计、服务设计、用户体验设计的初衷，无不是突破原有设计的界限，从新的维度建立新的愿景与使命</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">。</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">在可预见的未来，新的设计概念与设计方法将会层出不穷，以量变</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">或者质变</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">的方式</span><span class="ql-author-5022786">，</span><span class="ql-author-4782703">一步步改变直至革命现有设计的体系，大破大立般拥有新的假设，建立新秩序和方法。</span>
</p>

<p class="ql-long-4782703">
  <span class="ql-author-4782703">困于案几，也须记得遥望星空。</span>
</p>
